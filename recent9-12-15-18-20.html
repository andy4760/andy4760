How It Works

  • Enter numbers from 0-36 or "00" to track them
  • The tracker maintains the last 20 numbers entered
  • Recent 9 Logic: Checks if the entered number is in the most recent 9 numbers
  • Recent 12 Logic: Checks if the entered number is in the most recent 12 numbers
  • Recent 15 Logic: Checks if the entered number is in the most recent 15 numbers
  • Recent 18 Logic: Checks if the entered number is in the most recent 18 numbers
  • All 20 Logic: Checks if the entered number is in any of the 20 tracked numbers
  • Use Undo to revert the last action, or Reset to clear all data
