How It Works

  • Enter numbers from 0-36 or "00" to track them
  • The tracker maintains the last 24 numbers entered
  • 20 Entries: Shows the most recent 20 numbers in entry order (newest to oldest)
  • Recent 12 Logic: Checks if the entered number is in the most recent 12 numbers
  • Recent 18 Logic: Checks if the entered number is in the most recent 18 numbers
  • All 24 Logic: Checks if the entered number is in any of the 24 tracked numbers
  • Missing Numbers: Shows 14 numbers that haven't appeared in the last 24 numbers
  • Use Undo to revert the last action, or Reset to clear all data
